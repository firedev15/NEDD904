Solution 1

Scaffolded Goal Setting

A structured 10‑week pathway that helps learners set goals, monitor progress, and grow autonomy over time.

Why It Works

Goal setting boosts motivation for children, adolescents, and adults when support is gradually released and progress is visible.

Evidence‑aligned

Goal Types

Balance short‑term performance goals with long‑term learning goals.

Performance Goals

Weekly · Short‑term

Examples:

  • Complete pre‑class reading
  • Attempt a tutorial question

Learning Goals

Semester · Long‑term

Examples:

  • Cook a dish
  • Bake a pastry

Student Goal Tracker

Students can set their learning goal and weekly performance goals, then track progress over time.

Progress Snapshot

No goals tracked yet.

Student Comments

Teachers Remark

Space for teacher feedback, encouragement, and next steps.


Sample Feedback:
Week 4 update: Great progress on your weekly reading goals. Next week, try pairing your reading with a short reflection to deepen understanding.

Implementation Notes

This scaffold aligns with the progression:
demonstration → guided application → fading support → evaluation over a 10‑week course.
